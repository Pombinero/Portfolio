Different types of video games

Types of video games: FPS

FPS stands for "first-person shooter". It is a type of computer game in which the player aims and shoots at targets.

Types of video games: Sandbox

A game that allows the player access to the whole world at once, and allow them to change the world at will.

Types of video games: RPG

RPG stands for "role-playing game". A type of game in which players assume the roles of characters in a fictional setting.
